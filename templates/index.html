The Process

How IntervueX-AI
works in 4 steps

A transparent, deterministic pipeline from your profile to a ready-to-use interview sheet.

01

Enter Your Details

Provide your name, the target company, your role (e.g. "Gen AI Engineer"), and your experience level. That's all the engine needs.

02

Rules Engine Fires

Your role is normalized and fuzzy-matched to one of 16 known role profiles. Each profile carries a weighted list of topics and a difficulty distribution.

03

Questions Selected

Questions are drawn from a 114-item bank, topic by topic, respecting weighted counts and difficulty ratios — no duplicates, no gaps, always at least one per topic.

04

Your Interview is Ready

Browse questions by topic, reveal follow-up hints, see exactly which rules fired, and print your personalized question set instantly.
